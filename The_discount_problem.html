The discount problem: an analysis of a superstore chain

Using tableau for interactive visualizations and a story
of the sales, discount, and profit of a superstores chain

Data:

The data used was stored in an Excel file, ready to use, and was provided as part of the course "Data Visualization with Tableau Specialization" by the University of California, Davis.
