Impact of Civil Wars
throughout history

Using data scraping from Wikipedia in Python, data cleanup
and transformation in Excel, and visualization and presentation in Tableau.

Introduction:

This project started as an attempt to properly compare the effects of different civil wars throughout history, for this it uses a casualty/population ratio. To obtain the information required I scraped from Wikipedia the estimates of the total population of the countries and the deaths produced by each civil war from the 1500s up to the 2010s using Python. Next, I cleaned the data using Excel, simply changing a couple of countries' names from old to modern ones, and finally, I designed the visualization in Tableau using parameters and calculated fields to display the information on the map chart.
